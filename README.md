# Privacy Policy for Ride Share App


## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
- **Account Information**: When you register, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and password.
- **Profile Information**: We may collect additional details such   vehicle information (if applicable).

### 1.2 Location Data
- **Precise Location**: We collect your precise location when you use our services to find rides, offer rides, or when your location is shared in real-time during a ride.
- **Approximate Location**: We may collect approximate location information from your device when the app is running in the background.
